Resonance Logic

Most Important

Why is Aniline less basic than Ammonia?

Lone pair on N is delocalized into the benzene ring due to resonance, making it less available for donation.

Why is Phenol more acidic than Alcohol?

Phenoxide ion is stabilized by resonance, while Alkoxide ion is NOT.

Electronic Effects

Logic

Alkyl groups increase basicity of amines in gas phase.

Halogens increase acidity of carboxylic acids.

Conversion Pro-Tips
